Workout Plan

Select a category to view exercises that will help you maintain your fitness level and overall health.

Push-Ups

A classic upper body exercise targeting the chest, shoulders, and triceps while engaging the core.

High Intensity 15 reps × 3 sets

Overhead Shoulder Press

Strengthens the deltoids, triceps, and upper chest, enhancing pushing power and posture.

Moderate Weight 12 reps × 3 sets

Incline Dumbbell Press

Targets the upper chest and front delts to improve pressing strength and upper body mass.

Moderate to Heavy Weight 10 reps × 3 sets

Pull-Ups

A powerful upper-body move that works the lats, biceps, and shoulders for improved back strength.

High Intensity 8-10 reps × 3 sets

Bent-Over Rows

Engages the upper and middle back, biceps, and rear shoulders to build pulling strength.

Moderate to Heavy Weight 12 reps × 3 sets

Face Pulls

Targets the rear delts and traps, improving posture and shoulder stability.

Light to Moderate Weight 15 reps × 3 sets

Squats

Targets quads, glutes, hamstrings, and core. A compound powerhouse for building lower body strength.

Moderate to Heavy Weight 12 reps × 3 sets

Lunges

Improves balance and leg coordination while toning the quads and glutes.

Bodyweight / Moderate Weight 10 reps/leg × 3 sets

Calf Raises

Strengthens and tones calves while improving ankle stability and explosive power.

Bodyweight / Light Weight 20 reps × 3 sets

Interval Running

Alternate between sprints and jogs for improved stamina and calorie burn.

High Intensity 20-30 minutes

Cycling

Low-impact cardio that builds endurance and tones legs.

Moderate Intensity 30-45 minutes

Jump Rope

Boosts heart rate while improving footwork, coordination, and stamina.

High Intensity 5 rounds of 1 minute

Wellness Tips for Maintenance

Small, consistent changes make a big impact. Use these daily habits to fuel your progress!

Eat Smart

Prioritize veggies, lean proteins, and whole grains. Cut down on processed foods and added sugars.

Quality Sleep

Aim for 7-9 hours of quality sleep nightly to allow your body to recover and maintain hormonal balance.

Stay Hydrated

Sip water throughout the day to reduce hunger and keep your body running smoothly.

Relax & Recharge

Don’t let stress stall your goals — take short breaks, breathe deeply, or move your body gently to reset.
